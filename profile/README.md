# WeList
 
<img src="../welist-logo-icon.svg" width="320">

> Every idea has a place

WeList is a collaborative list-building platform where every idea has a place. We list ideas on boards, from thoughts to things, giving chaos structure and clarity.

https://welist.org

## Overview

For individuals and teams who want to organize and share knowledge efficiently, WeList is a collaborative list-building platform that turns ideas into structured, shareable lists, making it simple and intuitive to create, manage, and discover data.
